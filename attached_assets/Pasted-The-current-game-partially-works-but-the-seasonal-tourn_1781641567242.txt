The current game partially works, but the seasonal tournament progression is still broken. Do NOT rewrite the entire game. Fix and extend the existing systems while preserving all current functionality.

CRITICAL GOAL:
The game must simulate a complete competitive year automatically, with proper progression from Regional Leagues to Majors and finally The International.

====================================================
SEASON STRUCTURE
====================================================

Implement the following annual structure:

CYCLE 1
- January-March:
  Regional Leagues
  Regional Playoffs
- April:
  Major #1

CYCLE 2
- May-July:
  Regional Leagues
  Regional Playoffs
- August:
  Major #2

CYCLE 3
- September-October:
  Regional Leagues
  Regional Playoffs
- November:
  Major #3

END OF YEAR
- Determine The International participants
- Run The International
- Show season summary
- Start a new calendar year

====================================================
REGIONAL LEAGUES
====================================================

There are 4 regions:

- Europe
- Asia
- Americas
- Africa/Oceania

Each Regional League contains exactly 16 teams.

Regional League requirements:

- Use a true Round Robin format.
- Every team must play every other team exactly once.
- All matches are Best of 3.
- Each team therefore plays exactly 15 league matches.
- The league table must update after every match.

Display:

- wins
- losses
- map differential (if available)
- league position

The player-controlled team must participate normally.

====================================================
REGIONAL PLAYOFFS
====================================================

After each Regional League finishes:

- Generate Regional Playoffs.
- Use league standings to seed the playoffs.
- Determine the teams that qualify for the Major.

Playoffs should influence qualification and seasonal performance.

====================================================
MAJORS
====================================================

After ALL four regional playoffs are completed:

Automatically create a Major.

Major slots:

- Europe: 6 teams
- Asia: 4 teams
- Americas: 3 teams
- Africa/Oceania: 3 teams

Major requirements:

- Run as a complete tournament.
- Simulate all matches.
- Update player statistics.
- Update HLTV rankings.
- Award TI Points.

The player team must participate if qualified.

====================================================
TI POINTS SYSTEM
====================================================

Create a separate TI Points ranking.

This is NOT the HLTV ranking.

Award TI Points based on:

- Regional League performance
- Regional Playoff performance
- Major performance

Store TI Points throughout the entire season.

====================================================
THE INTERNATIONAL
====================================================

At the end of the third cycle:

Determine The International participants.

Qualification:

- 16 teams qualify directly through TI Points.
- 4 teams qualify through Regional Qualifiers.
- One Regional Qualifier winner per region.

Total teams:
20.

Tournament format:

Swiss Stage:
- 3 wins = advance.
- 3 losses = elimination.
- Teams play opponents with the same Swiss record.

Then:
- Playoffs.
- Determine the World Champion.

- Regional Leagues
- Regional Playoffs
- Majors
- TI Points
- The International
- HLTV Rankings
- Match History

No separate logic for the player team.
